While using the "Daxuesheng" APP, we may collect the following personal information: basic information such as name, nickname, and avatar; contact information such as phone number and email; device information such as device model, OS version, and IP address; location information for location-based campus services (subject to your authorization).

UGC 声明 UGC Statement
本产品存在用户生成内容（UGC）场景，采用百度敏感词智能校验 + 人工复审双重风控机制。用户发布的文本、图片等内容，先经过百度敏感词库实时过滤拦截违规词汇与违规图片，机器初筛拦截后，所有剩余内容再由运营管理员人工二次审核，实行先审核后展示机制，杜绝违法违规、低俗不良信息传播，保障平台内容安全合规。
This product involves User Generated Content (UGC) scenarios and adopts a dual risk-control mechanism of Baidu sensitive word intelligent verification + manual review. Text, images, and other content posted by users are first filtered in real-time by the Baidu sensitive word database to intercept prohibited words and images. After machine preliminary screening, all remaining content undergoes a second manual review by operations administrators. A "review before display" mechanism is implemented to prevent the spread of illegal, non-compliant, vulgar, or harmful information and ensure platform content safety and compliance.
